End of Term Examination Schedule

All students are expected to prepare thoroughly and report on time for all scheduled examinations.

The end of term examinations will commence from December 10th and will continue until December 20th, 2025. This is a critical period for all students as it will determine their academic progress and class placement for the next term.

Important Guidelines

  • All students must arrive at school 15 minutes before the examination starts
  • No student is allowed to enter the examination room after 10 minutes of the start time
  • Mobile phones and unauthorized materials are strictly prohibited
  • Students must bring their admission cards and writing materials
  • Malpractice of any kind will result in disciplinary action

Examination Schedule

Date Subject Time Duration
December 10 English Language 9:00 AM 2 hours
December 11 Mathematics 9:00 AM 2.5 hours
December 12 Science 9:00 AM 2 hours
December 13 Social Studies 9:00 AM 2 hours

Examination Centers

All examinations will be held in the school premises. Students will be assigned to examination centers based on their classes. The examination timetable will be posted on the school notice board and will also be sent to all students via email.

Results Release

The examination results will be released on December 28th, 2025. Students and parents are required to come to school for result collection. Results can also be accessed through the school portal using student login credentials.

Note:

Any student who is absent during any examination without a valid medical excuse will be marked absent and may be required to take a makeup examination with an additional fee.

We wish all students the very best in their examinations. For any queries or concerns, please contact the academic office. Remember, consistent hard work and dedication are keys to success.
