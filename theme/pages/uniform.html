School Uniform

Dress Code & Uniform Requirements

Uniform Introduction

At Excellence Academy, we believe that a proper school uniform promotes discipline, equality, and school identity. All students are required to wear the appropriate uniform for their category on school days. Please ensure your child is properly dressed according to the guidelines below.

Uniform by Category

Uniform Requirements by Category

Pre-Primary (Nursery)

Pre-Primary (Nursery 1-2)

Boys

  • Top: Light blue polo shirt with school logo
  • Bottom: Navy blue shorts
  • Footwear: Black canvas shoes with white socks
  • Accessories: Navy blue school cap (optional)

Nursery Boys Uniform

Girls

  • Top: Light blue polo shirt with school logo
  • Bottom: Navy blue skirt (knee-length)
  • Footwear: Black canvas shoes with white socks
  • Accessories: Navy blue hair ribbons or bands

Nursery Girls Uniform

Primary School

Primary School (Primary 1-6)

Boys

  • Accessories: Navy blue tie, school belt

Primary Boys Uniform

Girls

  • Top: White short-sleeve shirt with school badge
  • Bottom: Navy blue pinafore dress (knee-length)
  • Footwear: Black leather shoes with white socks

Sports & PE Uniform

General Guidelines

General Uniform Guidelines

Do's

  • Keep uniforms clean and well-pressed
  • Wear proper school shoes (no sandals or slippers)
  • Tuck in shirts properly
  • Maintain neat and tidy appearance
  • Wear ID cards at all times
  • Keep hair neat and well-groomed

Don'ts

  • No jewelry except wristwatches and simple earrings (girls)
  • No makeup or nail polish
  • No extreme hairstyles or hair coloring
  • No torn or modified uniforms
  • No mixing of uniform items from different categories
  • No non-uniform accessories

Important: Students who repeatedly fail to comply with uniform regulations may face disciplinary action. Parents are encouraged to ensure their children are properly dressed before leaving home.
