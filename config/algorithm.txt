## 4Ô∏è‚É£ FUNCTIONAL ALGORITHM FLOW

### üîπ STEP 1 ‚Äî HOMEPAGE DISPLAY
1. Fetch school details (logo, name, message, etc.)
2. Display welcome message from Director/CEO.
3. Show ‚ÄúQuick Links‚Äù to:
   - Admission
   - Fees
   - Timetable
   - Results
   - Contact

### üîπ STEP 2 ‚Äî ADMISSION PAGE
1. Show admission form (HTML form).
2. Allow applicants to fill details and submit.
3. Store applicant info in `students` table.
4. Generate admission number format as described in DB (`admission_number_format`).
5. Optionally auto-send admission letter PDF via email.

### üîπ STEP 3 ‚Äî FEES & PAYMENT PAGE
1. Fetch `fees` per class and term.
2. Display in table format with total payable.
3. Provide payment receipt upload/view.
4. Store record in `payments` table.
5. Optionally generate downloadable receipt.

### üîπ STEP 4 ‚Äî TIMETABLE PAGE
1. Show download links for `Lesson` and `Exam` timetables.
2. Use file upload from admin side (stored in `timetables` table).

### üîπ STEP 5 ‚Äî RESULTS & GRADES PAGE
1. Students can enter admission number.
2. Fetch results from `results` table.
3. Display grades, total, and remarks.
4. Allow download of result card or gradesheet.

### üîπ STEP 6 ‚Äî STAFF PAGE
1. Fetch all `staff` records.
2. Display profile pictures, names, qualifications, and subjects.

### üîπ STEP 7 ‚Äî GALLERY PAGE
1. Display high-resolution school and event pictures.
2. Stored in `gallery` table with captions.

### üîπ STEP 8 ‚Äî CONTACT PAGE
1. Show school address, contact details, social links.
2. Include a simple contact form (name, email, message).
